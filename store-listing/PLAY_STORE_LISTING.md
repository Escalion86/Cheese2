## Short Description (80 chars max)
Photo enhancement with playing card overlays. Capture, edit, and save.

## Full Description (4000 chars max)
Cheese2 is a unique photo enhancement app that lets you overlay playing card designs on your photos.

Features:
• Camera photo capture with live preview
• Playing card overlay effects (Ace of Hearts, King of Spades, and more)
• Adjustable card angle and scale
• Save enhanced photos to your device gallery
• Dark and Light theme support
• Push notification support
• Available in Russian and English

Perfect for magic performances, card trick enthusiasts, or anyone who wants to add a unique twist to their photos.
