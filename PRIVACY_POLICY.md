# Privacy Policy — Cheese2

Last updated: May 20, 2026

## Information We Collect

Cheese2 ("the App") accesses the following on your device:
- **Camera**: To capture photos
- **Media Library**: To save and access photos on your device
- **Device Motion**: For auto-rotate functionality

## How We Use Information

- Photos are stored locally on your device only
- No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted to external servers
- Push notification tokens are stored by Expo's push notification service

## Data Storage

- All photos remain on your device
- No data is uploaded to any server
- Push notification tokens are managed by Expo (https://expo.dev/privacy)

## Third-Party Services

- **Expo**: Push notifications via Expo's service (https://expo.dev/privacy)
- **Google Play Services**: App distribution and updates
