# Privacy Policy — Cheese2

**Last updated:** May 2026

## Overview

Cheese2 ("the App") is a mobile application for enhancing photos with playing card overlays. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use the App.

## Data Collection

The App collects the following data:

### Camera Access
- The App requests access to your device camera to capture photos.
- Photos are processed locally on your device.
- Photos you choose to save are stored in your device's media library.
- We do not upload, transmit, or store your photos on any external server.

### Media Library Access
- The App requests access to your device media library to save processed photos.
- We only write files that you explicitly choose to save.
- We do not read, collect, or transmit any existing media from your library.

### Device Motion
- The App uses device motion sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope) to detect device orientation for the auto-rotate feature.
- Motion data is processed locally and in real-time.
- We do not store, transmit, or share motion data.

### Push Notifications
- The App can register for push notifications via Expo.
- A device token (unique identifier) is generated and stored by the Expo push notification service.
- We do not use this token to personally identify you.

### Settings & Preferences
- App settings (theme, language, camera preferences) are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
- This data never leaves your device.
- No personal data is included in local storage.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- Your name or email address
- Your precise location
- Contacts, call logs, or browsing history
- Any other personal identifiers
- Usage analytics or crash reports

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **Expo (expo.dev)** — Build infrastructure and push notifications. Expo's privacy policy applies to push notification token management: https://expo.dev/privacy-policy
- **Google Play Services** — App distribution and in-app updates. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's Privacy

The App is directed at users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require login, registration, or any personal information.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. We do not transmit or store any user data on external servers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App's "About" screen and on this page.
